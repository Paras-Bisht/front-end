The problem with finding good movies and TV shows


Say you want to watch a movie tonight or binge-watch a sitcom this weekend. You search online using Google, YouTube, Reddit, Yahoo Answers, Quora, even Twitter, until you eventually choose a title you MIGHT like.

We do this a lot - waste a lot of time searching only to end up with an 'okay' movie or TV series recommendation.

But we know 'okay' isn't good enough. Your time is important, and watching movies and TV episodes that don’t really entertain or inspire you is a wasted evening. You need something that recommends a great movie every time.
